MOH-Approved COVID-19 Test Provider

A Healing Heart Medical Clinic is a MOH-approved COVID-19 test provider for polymerase chain reaction (PCR) and antigen rapid tests (ART).

We provide pre-departure tests (ART and PCR) for travel, pre-event test (ART) for attending local events, and ART test for pre-doctor consultation when you have flu-like symptoms (e.g., fever, cough, cold, runny nose).

Consulting Doctor for COVID-19 symptoms

Patients with COVID-19 symptoms (e.g., fever, cough, cold, sore throat, runny nose) will be asked to take ART at the clinic before consulting doctor. For everyone's safety, please kindly follow instructions by clinic staff and wear your mask at all times.

Click here and select "See Doctor (For Fever, Cough, Cold, Sore Throat, Runny Nose)" to book an appointment.

Updating National Immunisation Registry (NIR)

We can help individuals who have been vaccinated overseas with any vaccines approved under the WHO Emergency Use Listing (EUL), including booster shots, to get their vaccination records added into the National Immunisation Registry (NIR).

Please also note the Singapore government requires every resident, including those seeking work permit and long-term stay in Singapore, to receive minimum protection against COVID-19.

The minimum doses and type of vaccines required by MOH can be found on the Vaccine FAQs website, vaccine.gov.sg, and moh.gov.sg.

Those who are vaccinated overseas but their vaccines are not approved under WHO Emergency Use Listing (EUL) will need to take WHO EUL approved COVID-19 vaccination.

If you are required to take a serology blood test to prove that you have COVID-19 antibodies, please book appointment with us.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What happens when you are tested positive?

    After seeing doctor and prescribed medication, you are encouraged to stay at home, take your medication, and recover with minimal contact with outsiders. Please continue to self-test with ART kit during this recovery period.

    You are not encouraged to travel when you are tested COVID-19 positive. This is to minimise COVID-19 spread to your family members, loved ones and others.

  • Where can I notorise my vaccination certificate?

    There is no need to do so now. Most countries do not require vaccination certificate to travel.

  • Can I get a recovery memo or certificate?

    There is no more recovery memo or certificate issued by the Singapore government. You are encouraged to continue to self-test with ART kit during this recovery period. Please minimise contact with others to contain spread of the virus.

  • Do you still over PCR test?

    Yes, we still offer PCR test. However, the need for PCR test for travel or work purposes is no longer required. The expense of doing the PCR test is self-pay only. The price starts from $200 (as of 8 Sep 2023). You will get the results within 24 hours.
