MOH-Approved COVID-19 Test Provider

A Healing Heart Medical Clinic is a MOH-approved COVID-19 test provider for polymerase chain reaction (PCR) and antigen rapid tests (ART).

We provide pre-departure tests (ART and PCR) for travel, pre-event test (ART) for attending local events, and ART test for pre-doctor consultation when you have flu-like symptoms (e.g., fever, cough, cold, runny nose).

Travelling Overseas

When travelling overseas, please check the travel requirements needed before booking for a pre-departure ART or PCR test. For example, the testing window period (number of hours or days before departure), the vaccination status (fully, partially, or non-vaccinated), and the type of pre-departure test needed (ART or PCR) differ among countries.

Please use Singapore's Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA) website to check on these requiremetns before you travel. Do note that requirements may change over time.

When your travel ART and PCR test results are negative, we will generate a mandated digital health certificate that allows you to travel abroad. A PDF copy will be emailed to you.

Consulting Doctor with COVID-19 symptoms

Patients with COVID-19 symptoms (e.g., fever, cough, cold, runny nose) will be asked to take ART before consulting doctor. For everyone's safety, please kindly follow instructions by clinic staff and wear your mask at all times.

Updating National Immunisation Registry

We can help individuals who have been vaccinated overseas with any vaccines approved under the WHO Emergency Use Listing (EUL), including booster shots, to get their vaccination records added into the National Immunisation Registry (NIR).

Please also note the number of vaccination doses required by MOH to be considered as fully vaccinated. Download the document.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What happens when you are tested positive?

    Doctor will assess if you are well, have mild symptoms, or unwell. You will be placed under protocol one or two. For more details, please visit www.covid.gov.sg.

    You are not allowed to travel. The digital health certificate will not be issued when you are tested positive.

  • Where can I notorise my vaccination certificate?

    If you are vaccinated in Singapore and need to show proof of your vaccination when travelling abroad, please go to www.notarise.gov.sg to download your notorised vaccination certificate.

  • Can I get a recovery memo or certificate?

    You can apply for a digital copy of your recovery certificate via www.notarise.gov.sg if you had previously tested positive for COVID-19 at a professionally administered or supervised health provider in Singapore.

  • What if I do not receive my PCR results on time?

    Please text us via WhatsApp (6962 1362) if you do not receive your PCR results within 24 hours (for normal speed PCR test), and 6 hours (for express speed PCR test).
