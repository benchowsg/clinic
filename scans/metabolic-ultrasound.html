Metabolic Support Imaging: Beyond the Scale

Heart health is a systemic issue. We focus on the organs that drive your metabolism—the Liver, Kidneys, and Vascular tree—to identify the "silent" markers of metabolic stress that standard blood tests often miss.

Hepatobiliary (HBS) Scan: The Metabolic Engine

The liver is your primary site for glucose and lipid processing. Metabolic Associated Fatty Liver Disease (MAFLD) is often the first sign of insulin resistance. Our HBS scans visualize fatty infiltration (steatosis) and structural changes, allowing for intervention long before it leads to permanent scarring or heart complications.

Carotid Intima-Media Thickness (CIMT)

The neck arteries provide a window into your global stroke risk. Unlike a standard scan that only looks for blockages, CIMT measures the microscopic thickness of the artery walls. This "Vascular Age" assessment tells us if systemic inflammation is already causing structural changes to your arteries.

Renal (KUB) & Renal Artery Doppler

High blood sugar and pressure place immense strain on the kidneys. We combine structural KUB scans with Renal Doppler to measure blood flow resistance. This catches early signs of "Metabolic Friction"—where the heart has to work harder because the renal blood supply is under pressure.

Clinical Insight

"The kidneys and heart operate in a constant feedback loop. By visualizing renal artery resistance, we can identify why blood pressure remains 'resistant' despite standard medication."
