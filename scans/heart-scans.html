Advanced Heart Scans

Seeing is knowing. We use state-of-the-art imaging to look past traditional risk factors and visualize your actual arterial health.

CT Coronary Angiogram (CTA)

The CT Angiogram is the "gold standard" for non-invasive heart imaging. Unlike a standard stress test which only looks for severe blockages, the CTA allows us to see soft plaque—the unstable buildup that often causes sudden cardiac events.

  • 3D mapping of coronary arteries
  • Identification of narrowings (Stenosis)
  • Guided by Dr. Gerald’s precision protocols

The Clinical "Aha!"

"Many patients with 'normal' cholesterol scores have significant soft plaque. CTA allows us to find it 10 years before it becomes a heart attack."

Coronary Calcium Scoring

This is a fast, low-dose scan that acts as a "Risk Thermometer." It measures the amount of calcified (hard) plaque in your vessel walls.

A Score of Zero is a powerful indicator of low cardiac risk, while higher scores allow us to aggressively manage your metabolic health before symptoms start.

Echocardiogram (Heart Ultrasound)

Using real-time sound waves, we watch your heart in motion. We assess how your heart muscle relaxes and contracts, and ensure your valves are functioning without leaks or stiffness.

This is essential for patients experiencing shortness of breath, palpitations, or those with long-term high blood pressure.
