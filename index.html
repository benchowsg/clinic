Who We Are

Advanced Physicians

Led by Dr. Gerald Thang, a Principal Physician with a focus on Internal Medicine and Cardiology, A Healing Heart Medical Clinic is a dedicated clinic for complex diagnostic care in Singapore.

We focus on evaluating evolving or medically complex symptoms, such as undifferentiated chest pain, chronic fatigue, and metabolic instability, that require more time and depth than a standard GP consultation.

As the intermediate care bridge between general practice and specialists, our clinic provides personalised clinical assessments and long-term management for chronic conditions like hypertension, hyperlipidemia, heart failure, and diabetes.

Equipped with in-clinic rapid diagnostics, we provide immediate clarity for acute cardiac concerns, ensuring patients receive evidence-based answers and structured recovery pathways without unnecessary referral delays.
