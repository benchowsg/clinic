We Are

Advanced Physicians

Welcome to A Healing Heart Medical Clinic where your healing starts here.

We belong to a small but growing group of Advanced Physicians providing intermediate care to patients who do not quite need tertiary care and yet require more individualised attention.

Our team is dedicated to providing you with comprehensive services that will benefit your health, well-being and post-hospitalisation recovery.

With our streamlined registration system and rapid diagnostic procedures, we have more time to focus on optimising care plans and connecting you to resources that will set you on a healing path.

F.A.Q.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need to make an appointment?

    Yes, you need to make an appointment at least one working day in advance. To make an appointment, please click here. We will then contact you to confirm your appointment.

  • How do I make payment?

    You can use cashless payment like PayNow, PayLah, Nets, etc. Cash and credit card payments like Visa and Mastercard are also accepted.

  • Can I arrive later than my appointment time?

    We encourage you to arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment. If you arrive late, priority will be given to another patient.

  • Do you have online consultation?

    We do not provide online consultation to new patients. Online consultation, also known as video conference, is only to review existing patients whose health is stable and does not require physical examination.

  • If I have a sudden onset of chest pain, can I come to your clinic?

    No, we are not an acute resuscitation centre. All emergency cases should go to the A & E department in hospitals.
