The Cycle of Breathlessness

We use the "Rusty Pipe" logic for your lungs. When airways are blocked or inflamed, it creates a chain reaction that exhausts your entire system.

1. The "Spark" (Triggers)

Dust or pollution starts a hidden fire of swelling in your airways.

2. The "Squeeze" (Tight Pipes)

The muscles around your air-pipes tighten like a clenching fist, making them narrow.

3. The "Clog" (Mucus)

Thick mucus further blocks the pipes, causing that heavy chest feeling.

4. Heart Pressure

Your heart has to "sprint" to pump oxygen, eventually exhausting the muscle.
