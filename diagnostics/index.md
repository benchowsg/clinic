## 4. Statutory Medical Examinations & Public Subsidies

A Healing Heart Medical Clinic is fully integrated with Singapore's National Electronic Health Record (NEHR) and HealthHub frameworks. We are a non-GST registered clinic, passing a direct 9% savings to our patients on all clinical and diagnostic fees.

### National Subsidy Integrations:
* **CHAS Accredited:** Blue, Orange, Green, Merdeka Generation, and Pioneer Generation subsidies are automatically computed for both acute symptom consultations and chronic metabolic management.
* **MediSave Approved:** Applicable for the outpatient management of approved chronic conditions (Hypertension, Hyperlipidemia, Diabetes, and Heart Failure) under the CDMP framework.
* **CDC Vouchers:** Fully accepted for clinical consults, rapid diagnostic testing, and in-clinic medications.

### Statutory Licensing Checkups Available:
We provide fast, compliant medical testing and official documentation for:
* Ministry of Manpower (MOM) Work Permits, S-Pass, and E-Pass screenings.
* Immigration & Checkpoints Authority (ICA) Long-Term Visit Passes and Student Passes.
* Land Transport Authority (LTA) vocational and driving license medical renewals.

## 5. Frequently Asked Diagnostic Questions (FAQ)

### Why does an intermediate care clinic take longer than a standard GP visit?
Standard primary care focuses on rapid, high-volume acute issues (like flu or cough). A Healing Heart Medical handles complex, multi-system issues like evolving chest pain or chronic metabolic instability. Dr. Gerald Thang allocates 30 to 60 minutes for complex diagnostic consultations to map out historical trends, genetics, and immediate bio-marker data.

### Do I need an official polyclinic or specialist referral to book a cardiac diagnostic test?
No. You do not need any referral letter. As an independent intermediate care clinic, patients can self-refer and walk in directly for advanced lipid audits, 15-minute troponin testing, or chronic care tracking.

### How are my diagnostic results shared securely with other hospitals?
Our clinic uploads your critical health records, chronic management plans, and national immunizations directly to the National Electronic Health Record (NEHR). This ensures that if you ever visit a public hospital emergency department (SGH, TTSH, KTPH, NUH), the emergency physicians will instantly see your updated cardiac baselines from our clinic.
