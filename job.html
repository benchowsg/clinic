Are you the one?

We are an intermediate care medical clinic group seeking professionally trained enrolled or registered nurses with at least two years of relevant working experiences to join us as full-time staff.

If you are a committed professional who takes pride in providing patient care and have the empathy to connect with patients and their family members, we want you!

Enrolled Nurse or Registered Nurse

Job description

  • Must be able to draw blood and perform vaccination on patients
  • Must be able to perform blood glucose monitoring, resting ECG, swab tests duties
  • Manage patient's appointments, patient care and counselling
  • Handle registration, enquiries, billings, claims (CHAS, Medisave, Insurance), stock taking of medication and inventory
  • Liaise with relevant drugs, laboratory and medical partners
  • Dispense medication with supervision from doctor
  • Assist doctor and be a chaperon when required
  • Maintain a clean and safe clinic environment

Job Requirements

  • Singaporean or Permanent Resident
  • At least 2 years of recent working experience at a medical clinic or a hospital setting
  • Comfortable to use computer to process claims and documents
  • Familiarity with clinic management system is advantageous
  • Ability to submit CHAS, Medisave and insurance claims are advantageous
  • Good communication skills
  • Able to speak and write in English and a second language preferred
  • Able to speak in dialects is advantageous
  • Able to work in a team and is responsible
  • Good customer service skills
  • Able to start immediately or on short notice

Education Level

  • At least Nitec in Nursing, or Diploma in Nursing
  • Registered with Singapore Nursing Board
  • BCLS / AED certified
  • Certificate in Phlebotomy (advantageous)

Working Hours

  • Monday to Friday: 9am to 6pm (with lunch break from 12.30pm to 2pm)
  • Saturday: 9am to 1pm
  • Rest on Sunday and Public Holiday

Staff Benefits Include

  • Off-day scheme for hardworking staff
  • AWS and performance bonus
  • Medical and dental benefits
