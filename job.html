Full-Time Registered Nurse

Plant your roots and grow your career

If you're looking for stability, career progression, and a close-knit team, our full-time roles offer a chance to truly invest in your future and ours.

Why Join Us Full-Time?

  • Financial Stability: Competitive salary and a monthly performance-based bonus.
  • Comprehensive Family Health Support: Medical and dental care for you and your immediate family members.
  • Goodwill Leave: Up to 2 days of Goodwill Leave annually for personal time or professional development.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Perform blood draws, vaccinations, and clinical procedures (ECG, swab tests).
  • Manage patient registration, appointments, and care coordination.
  • Handle CHAS, MediSave, and insurance claims and general enquiries.
  • Dispense medication (under doctor’s supervision) and assist during consultations.

Working Hours:

  • Mon to Fri: 9:00am – 6:00pm (Lunch: 12:30pm – 2:00pm)
  • Saturday: 9:00am – 1:00pm
  • Closed on Sundays and Public Holidays.

Requirements & Experience:

  • Nitec or Diploma in Nursing, registered with SNB.
  • Minimum 2 years of full-time clinic or hospital experience.
  • Valid BCLS / AED certification is required.

Locum Nurse Opportunities

Maximize Your Flexibility and Earnings

If you are seeking **maximum control over your schedule** and a way to apply your skills without the constraints of a full-time commitment, our locum roles offer excellent pay and professional autonomy.

Compensation & Requirements

Important: Contract Status & Compensation Transparency
  • Hourly Rate: **S$23.00 to S$28.00** per hour.
  • Contract Type: This is a **Contract for Service (Self-Employed Person - SEP)** engagement.
  • Benefits: The hourly rate is all-inclusive. You will **NOT** receive employer CPF, paid annual/sick leave, or other statutory employee benefits.

Availability Requirements

  • Must be able to accept full-day weekday assignments.
  • **Consistent availability on Saturdays is essential.**

Eligibility:

  • Nitec or Diploma in Nursing, registered with SNB.
  • Applicants must have been actively working as a nurse within the last three months.
