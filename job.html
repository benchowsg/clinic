Job Vacancy

We are an intermediate care medical clinic group looking to expand our team with committed and qualified Enrolled Nurses,Registered Nurses, and Locum Nurses. If you have at least two years of relevant healthcare experience and are passionate about patient care, we’d love to meet you.

Join us in delivering compassionate, quality care in a supportive and growth-oriented environment.

Position: Enrolled Nurse / Registered Nurse

Key Responsibilities:

  • Perform blood draws, vaccinations, and clinical procedures (e.g. blood glucose monitoring, ECG, swab tests)
  • Manage patient registration, appointments, and care coordination
  • Handle CHAS, MediSave, and insurance claims, as well as general enquiries and billing
  • Dispense medication (under doctor’s supervision) and assist during consultations
  • Liaise with external laboratories and medical suppliers
  • Maintain a clean, safe, and patient-friendly clinical environment

Requirements:

  • Comfortable with using clinic management software and digital systems
  • Familiarity with claims processing (CHAS, MediSave, insurance) is an advantage
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills
  • Fluent in English; proficiency in a second language or dialect is a bonus
  • Reliable, team-oriented, and able to work independently when needed
  • Able to start work immediately or within short notice

Education & Certification:

  • Minimum: Nitec or Diploma in Nursing
  • Registered with the Singapore Nursing Board
  • Certified in BCLS / AED
  • Certificate in Phlebotomy (preferred but not required)

Experience:

  • Minimum 2 years of full-time experience in a clinic, mobile clinic, or hospital setting
  • Minimum 2 years of practical experience in blood-taking procedures

Working Hours:

  • Monday to Friday: 9.00am – 6.00pm (Lunch break: 12.30pm – 2.00pm)
  • Saturday: 9.00am – 1.00pm
  • Closed on Sundays and Public Holidays

Staff Benefits:

  • Monthly performance-based bonus
  • Medical and dental benefits

Clinic Location:

  • Walking distance from Aljunied and Paya Lebar MRT stations
  • Located along Sims Avenue, in front of a bus stop (Bus numbers: 2, 2A, 13, 21, 26, 40, 51, 67, 137, 853M)

Position: Locum Nurse

Key Requirements & Contract Status:

We are seeking Locum Nurses (RNs/ENs) for a Contract for Service engagement. Please review the following terms carefully before applying.

Compensation & Status:

  • Hourly Rate: S$23.00 to S$28.00 per hour (based on experience and qualifications).
  • Contract Type: This is a Contract for Service (Self-Employed Person - SEP). You are not covered by the Employment Act.
  • CPF & Benefits: The hourly rate is all-inclusive. You will **NOT** receive employer CPF contributions, paid annual leave, paid sick leave, or other statutory employee benefits.
  • Medisave: SEPs who are Singapore Citizens/PRs are personally responsible for making mandatory CPF MediSave contributions in accordance with IRAS requirements.

Availability Requirement:

  • Must be able to accept assignments for a minimum of 2 weekdays per week.
  • Consistent availability to accept assignments on Saturdays is essential to meet our core clinic operational needs.
