Full-Time Registered Nurse

Plant your roots and grow your career

If you're looking for stability, career progression, and a close-knit team, our full-time roles offer a chance to truly invest in your future and ours.

Why Join Us Full-Time?

  • Financial Stability: Enjoy guaranteed hours, a competitive salary structure, and a monthly performance-based bonus.
  • Comprehensive Family Health Support: Access to medical and dental care, plus consultation with a doctor and medication coverage for yourself and your immediate family members.
  • Goodwill Leave (Time for Life & Growth): Besides statutory leave, we provide up to 2 days of Goodwill Leave annually for family time or professional development.
  • Statutory Support: Access to required statutory annual leave, medical leave, childcare leave and maternity leave.
  • Supportive Team: Be a core member of our staff with full access to internal resources and mentorship.

Key Role Summary & Location

About the Role: Provide high-quality patient care in a dynamic environment, assisting the doctor with clinical procedures, nurse counselling, and managing patient coordination.

Clinic Location: Conveniently located within walking distance of Aljunied and Paya Lebar MRT stations, with a bus stop right in front.

Full Job Details

Working Hours:

  • Monday to Friday: 9:00am – 6:00pm (Lunch break: 12:30pm – 2:00pm)
  • Saturday: 9:00am – 1:00pm
  • Closed on Sundays and Public Holidays.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Perform blood draws, vaccinations, and clinical procedures (e.g., blood glucose monitoring, ECG, swab tests).
  • Manage patient registration, appointments, and care coordination.
  • Handle CHAS, MediSave, and insurance claims, as well as general enquiries and billing.
  • Dispense medication (under doctor’s supervision) and assist during consultations.
  • Liaise with external laboratories and medical suppliers.
  • Maintain a clean, safe, and patient-friendly clinical environment.

Education & Certification:

  • Minimum: Nitec or Diploma in Nursing.
  • Valid, Registration with the Singapore Nursing Board.
  • Valid, Certified in BCLS / AED.
  • Valid, Certificate in Phlebotomy (preferred but not required).

Experience:

  • Minimum 2 years of full-time experience in a clinic or hospital setting.
  • Minimum 2 years of practical experience in blood-taking procedures.

General Requirements:

  • Comfortable with using clinic management software and digital systems.
  • Familiarity with claims processing (CHAS, MediSave, insurance) is an advantage.
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to document triage properly is appreciated.
  • Fluent in English; proficiency in a second language or dialect is a bonus.
  • Reliable, team-oriented, and able to work independently when needed.
  • Able to start work immediately or within short notice.

Locum Nurse Opportunities

Maximize Your Flexibility and Earnings

If you are seeking **maximum control over your schedule** and a way to apply your skills without the constraints of a full-time commitment, our locum roles offer excellent pay and professional autonomy.

Why Join Us as a Locum?

  • Competitive Hourly Rate: Earn between **S$23.00 to S$28.00 per hour**, based on your experience and specific qualifications.
  • Total Flexibility: Choose assignments that fit your life. You manage your schedule and take on shifts as a self-employed professional.
  • Essential Experience: Gain exposure to **intermediate care services**, including preventive medicine and chronic care management.

Availability Requirements

We require locum nurses to ensure consistent coverage for our patients. Please ensure you can commit to the following:

  • Weekdays: Must be able to accept full-day weekday assignments.
  • Saturdays: Consistent availability to accept assignments on Saturdays is essential.

Important: Contract Status & Compensation Transparency

  • Contract Type: This is a Contract for Service (Self-Employed Person - SEP) engagement.
  • Compensation is All-Inclusive: You will **NOT receive** employer CPF contributions, paid annual leave, paid sick leave, or other statutory employee benefits.
  • Nationality: Only Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents are eligible.
  • Experience: Applicants must have an active role as a nurse in clinic or hospital within the last three months.

Education & Certification:

  • Minimum: Nitec or Diploma in Nursing.
  • Valid, Registration with the Singapore Nursing Board.
  • Valid, Certified in BCLS / AED.
