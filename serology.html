COVID-19 Serology Test and Update NIR

For vaccinated individuals

With overseas COVID-19 vaccination records

COVID-19 vaccination will be a condition for the approval or grant of new long-term passes, work passes, student passes, and permanent residence from 1st February 2022. This condition applies to those who are seeking to renew their existing work passes.

For more details, please visit the Ministry of Manpower's (MOM) website.

Serology test to update NIR

Step 1: Book your appointment online.

Step 2: On the day of appointment, please bring your (a) long-term pass, work pass, blue or pink NRIC, and (b) your original vaccination record.

Step 3: Please wear short-sleeve shirt or T-shirt to draw blood. Blood sample is sent to the lab for testing.

Important: Pass holders must bring their original vaccination records. We will not proceed for those who are unable to provide valid documents.

Price: $72 (no GST). This is valid from 3rd January to 31st March 2021.

This test applies to Singaporeans with overseas vaccination records who need to update the National Immunisation Registry (NIR).

We will update those with positive result from the Serology test. Your vaccination status will also be reflected in TraceTogether a few days later.
