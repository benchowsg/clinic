Guide to HealthHub & NEHR Integration

Empowering patients with digital access to their cardiac and chronic care records.

As part of Singapore's national health ecosystem, A Healing Heart Medical is fully integrated with HealthHub and the National Electronic Health Record (NEHR). This ensures your medical history is securely accessible to you and your authorized care team.

What you can find on HealthHub

Patients of Dr. Gerald Thang can access the following data through the HealthHub app or portal:

  • Medical Records: Clinical summaries and specialist memos from your consultations.
  • Lab & Test Results: Track cholesterol (lipids), blood glucose (HbA1c), and other cardiac markers.
  • Medication History: View prescriptions issued by our clinic for your chronic disease management.
  • Vaccinations: NIR-synced records for Flu, Pneumococcal, or COVID-19 shots.

Healthier SG & Chronic Care

If you have enrolled with us under Healthier SG, use HealthHub to:

  • Access your Personalised Health Plan.
  • Manage appointments for annual health check-ins.
  • Monitor CHAS and MediSave balances for outpatient chronic treatments.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for results to appear?

Data usually syncs within 24 to 48 hours after your visit. If records are missing after 3 days, please contact our clinic assistant.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted and managed under Health Information Bill (HIB) guidelines. You can manage your NEHR access preferences within the app settings.
