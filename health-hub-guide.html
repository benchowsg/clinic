Your Precision Heart Data, Digitally Integrated

At A Healing Heart Medical, led by Dr. Gerald Thang, we move beyond basic screenings. By integrating with the National Electronic Health Record (NEHR), we ensure your advanced biomarkers and metabolic data are securely accessible whenever you need them.

1. Tracking Beyond the Basics

Standard screenings often miss the "Hidden Risks." Via the HealthHub App, you can monitor the specific metrics we use to protect your heart:

  • Advanced Lipid Insights: View your synced results for Triglycerides and HDL to calculate your "Small Dense LDL" risk.
  • Metabolic Markers: Track HbA1c and Kidney Function to see how your "24-Hour Shield" is performing.
  • Medication Synchronization: Ensure your long-acting prescriptions (Steady-State therapy) are updated across your healthcare team.

To view: Open HealthHub > Services > Health Records

2. Healthier SG: Targeting the "Asian Phenotype"

As your Healthier SG partner, Dr. Thang focuses on more than just weight. We prioritize the Waist-to-Height Ratio (WtHR)—a superior predictor of heart health for Singaporeans.

  • The 0.5 Goal: Access your Personalized Health Plan to see if your WtHR is within the "Healthy Zone" (under 0.5).
  • Visceral Fat Management: Monitor the clinical steps we’ve set to reduce internal organ inflammation.
  • Subsidised Care: Seamlessly book your annual check-ins and heart-safe vaccinations.

3. Seamless Subsidy Management

Focus on your recovery, not the paperwork. HealthHub simplifies your clinic experience:

  • CHAS & MediSave: Check your real-time balances for chronic disease management and outpatient claims.
  • Digital Transparency: Access your invoices and memos from A Healing Heart Medical for insurance or personal tracking.
