The Weight-Sugar Cycle

High blood sugar and weight gain are deeply linked through Insulin Resistance. When insulin levels remain high, the body enters a "fat storage" mode, making weight loss nearly impossible despite dieting.

Long-Term Effects of Poor Control

Uncontrolled sugar leads to microvascular damage, affecting the heart, kidneys, and nervous system. Early, systemic intervention is the only way to ensure long-term organ preservation.

Our goal is to break this cycle by improving insulin sensitivity, not just masking the symptoms with increasing doses of medication.
