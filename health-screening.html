Precision Health Screening: Insights for Optimised Health & Recovery

Bridging the Gap: Specialised Screening for Advanced Wellness

Are you ready to move beyond the basic check-ups?

At A Healing Heart Medical, our focus is on finding the answers others miss. We specialise in Cardiology, Regenerative Medicine, and Intermediate Care. This means our health screening is not just a test, but it is the first step in a highly personalised medical strategy designed to achieve optimal health, manage complex symptoms, and ensure lasting recovery.

We translate advanced diagnostics into a clear, actionable plan, positioning ourselves as the Principal Physician who coordinates your recovery needs across multiple specialties.

Your Screening. Your Strategy. Under Our Care.

Regenerative Medicine Cardiology Complex Rapid Care

Regenerative Medicine & Wellness Screening Packages

Our regenerative medicine and wellness screening packages focus on restoring balance, supporting hormonal health, and optimizing vitality. Designed for those who want to look, feel, and function at their best.

Cardiology Screening Packages

Our focus on cardiology care includes prevention. We look beyond basic blood pressure and cholesterol numbers. Our advanced screening and imaging are designed to detect risks early, protect your most vital organ, and provide you with actionable certainty about your long-term heart health.

Package Includes
Cardiack Risk Health Pack
Starts from $278		 Essential screening for anyone with cholesterol, high blood pressure, and diabetes concerns or a family history of heart disease.
Cardiac Assurance Health Pack
Starts from $538		 Steps up for those in need of a functional check on their heart's performance and structure.
Definitive Cardiac Health Pack
Starts from $638		 For high-risk individuals or those requiring definitive clarity on silent blockages.

Focused Cardiac Diagnostic Services

These services are typically used for targeted investigation based on specific symptoms, often integrated with your Principal Physician care.

Service Focus Who It Benefits
24-Hour Holter Monitoring For patients experiencing intermittent symptoms like palpitations, lightheadedness, or heart flutters that are unpredictable and difficult to catch during a clinic visit.
Rapid Cardiac Care For patients presenting with acute concerns like new or worsening chest pain, severe breathlessness, or acute fatigue, where timely clarity is critical.

Complex Rapid Care and Principal Physician

The Principal Physician is the core of our Intermediate Care. This is the senior doctor who takes on the responsibility to work with you and his team in coordinating all specialised aspects of your health.

We focus on complex patients who need rapid evaluation and seamless guidance through recovery after a major illness, hospital stay, or while dealing with chronic symptoms.

Service Focus Who It Benefits
Post-Hospital and Major Recovery Care Recently discharged patients or those recovering from major events (e.g., heart event, surgery, severe COVID-19, respiratory issues.)
Complex Case Management Patients experiencing prolonged fatigue, breathlessness, chronic pain, or complex symptoms not resolved by general care, or those needing urgent clarity.
