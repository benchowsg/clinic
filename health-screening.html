Individualised and Targeted Health Screening

We believe health screening is one of the core pillars of preventive medicine.

Health Screening Packages

We have customised three essential health screening packages and two packages with cardiac screening. Please refer to the table below.

Essential Titanium

Health is important to all of us. We have designed Titanium screening to be accessible to all of us and yet still provides the basic essential screening we require. Titanium screening consists of blood tests, urine tests and also a chest X-Ray to evaluate your health.

Essential Gold

Gold screening is a step-up from Titanium screening. It has cancer markers, hormone testing and more comprehensive kidney tests. An ECG is also part of Gold screening to give a better insight into your cardiac health.

Essential Platinum

Platinum screening offers a full suite of essential health screening and cancer markers. It also includes an ultrasound prostate (for gentlemen) or PAP smear â€“ Thin Prep (for ladies). Platinum screening gives you a broad overview and yet detailed information about your health.

Cardiac Executive

Cardiac Executive is the first of our three cardiac health screenings. This screening is selected for busy executives with a focus on your cardiac health. From extensive cardiac blood screening to a treadmill stress test, this allows us to investigate any cardiac ischemia and the overall conditioning of your body.

Cardiac Premium

Cardiac Premium is a step-up for cardiac screening with the addition of Exercise Stress Echocardiogram. A stress echocardiogram offers additional insight into the real time function and structure of the heart. It also detects heart ischemia without the risk of radiation. Together with cardiac blood tests, Cardiac Premium will provide a more complete cardiac evaluation for you.

Cardiac Excellence

Cardiac Excellence is the most comprehensive set of cardiac evaluation that is technologically available at this point in time. Cardiac Excellence includes a Magnetic Resonance Scan (MRA) of coronary arteries that is radiation free and does not require any contrast. This will not require any exercises during the testing as well. If desired, we are able to replace MRA with a CT coronary angiogram at no further charge.

*All Health Screening combinations are inclusive of 2 free (pre-test and post-test) consultations where our advanced physicians will go through your concerns in detail.

Individualised Annual Screening

For those who want individualised screening, we are happy to guide you through a selection of health screening tests.
