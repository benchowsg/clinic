Health Screening: Common Questions

Expert insights on choosing the right assessment for your lifestyle and age.

Which screening package should I choose?

The Foundational pack is best for annual tracking for those under 35. We recommend the Wellness pack for those 35+ to screen for age-related risks, and the Regenerative pack for those focused on peak performance and longevity.

What is a CT Calcium Score?

A CT Calcium Score is a specialized scan that measures calcified plaque in your heart arteries. It is a powerful predictor of heart attack risk, often identifying issues that standard cholesterol tests might miss.

How long does a health screening session take?

Most screenings are completed within 60 to 90 minutes. For packages including imaging or advanced cardiac markers, we ensure a streamlined process so you can get back to your day quickly. Thus pre-booking your appointment 2 weeks in advance will allow us to coordinate these services.

Do I need to fast before my screening?

Yes, for accurate blood glucose and cholesterol results, a 8-10 hour fast is required. You may drink plain water, but please avoid food, alcohol, coffee, tea, caffeniated or sweetened beverages.
