Clinic Nurse (Full-Time)

Permanent / Full-Time Near Aljunied MRT S$3,100 - S$4,100 / month

Company Overview

Set a different pace in your career. We welcome all qualified nurses to apply. As an intermediate care medical clinic, we provide a higher level of care, focusing on preventive and metabolic health, chronic cardiology care, and post-hospitalisation recovery. Located conveniently near Aljunied MRT.

Job Summary

You will deliver proactive, patient-centered nursing care in an outpatient clinic, focusing on preventive health, chronic disease management, and clinical excellence to support optimal patient outcomes.

Responsibilities
  • Conduct comprehensive patient assessments by taking vital signs, medical histories, and performing screenings to identify potential health issues early.
  • Educate and counsel patients on chronic disease management, lifestyle modifications, and preventive health.
  • Administer medications and perform clinical procedures following evidence-based protocols.
  • Perform blood taking (phlebotomy), vaccinations, ECGs, and wound dressing procedures.
  • Collaborate with physicians and staff to coordinate care and enhance patient experience.
  • Prepare and maintain clinical equipment; support basic insurance/billing claims processing.
Required Competencies & Certifications
  • Registered with the Singapore Nursing Board.
  • Minimum Nitec or Diploma in Nursing with at least 2 years of experience.
  • Valid BCLS / AED certification.
Preferred Qualifications (Bonus)
  • Certificate in Phlebotomy.
  • Prior experience in an outpatient clinic or handling claims processing.
  • Proficiency in a second language or dialect for optimal patient communication.
