Metabolic Health Coach & Coordinator

Self-Employed / Contractor Flexible Location / Remote-Friendly Metabolic Weight Management

Role Overview

At A Healing Heart Medical Clinic, we are re-framing how stubborn weight and cardiovascular risks are managed. We are looking for an independent, self-motivated Metabolic Health Coach & Coordinator to operate alongside our care network. This role allows for location flexibility, utilizing digital tracking and hybrid touchpoints to help patients break through metabolic friction.

Job Summary

You will directly advise and track patients navigating our metabolic stewardship programs. Your primary focus is translating clinical targets (like lowering visceral fat and stabilizing insulin pathways) into actionable, sustainable everyday lifestyle roadmaps.

Key Responsibilities
  • Conduct personalized coaching consultations to address dietary triggers, inflammatory profiles, and lifestyle patterns.
  • Monitor and audit patient composition trends using modern indexes like Relative Fat Mass (RFM) and Waist-to-Height ratios.
  • Educate patients on the systemic connections between deep visceral fat, insulin spikes, and overall vascular health.
  • Coordinate care checkpoints with our clinical partners to track patient long-term wellness data.
Core Requirements
  • Recognized certification or diploma in Health Coaching, Nutrition, Dietetics, Sports Science, or Allied Health frameworks.
  • Strong comprehension of metabolic conditions, insulin resistance parameters, and weight-management biology.
  • Excellent communication skills with the capability to manage client portfolios independently and professionally.
  • Stable internet access and setup for remote video/digital consultation touchpoints.
Arrangement Details
Location Flexibility This position features a highly flexible location layout. Consultations and patient monitoring can be performed via remote digital systems, backed by occasional hybrid alignment meetings.
Contractual Autonomy Operate on a freelance / self-employed contract structure. Perfect for established coaches looking to align with a data-driven clinical medical ecosystem.
Interested in a Partnership?

Please submit your professional resume, coaching credentials, and a brief overview of your approach to metabolic weight stewardship to our operations team.
