Heart Health Screening

As an intermediate care medical clinic, we bridge between general practice and specialty care. Our medical director, Dr Gerald Thang, has over 15 years of experiences, including cardiology specialist outpatient clinic. All Heart Health Screenings include a pre-assessment consultation and medical report review with Dr Thang.

For our heart health screening, we provide the option for you to choose from Treadmill Stress Test, 2D Echocardiogram, Exercise Stress Echocardiogram, Computed Tomography (CT) Coronary Angiography, and Cardiac Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)

Together with essential blood tests for all heart health screenings, including high-sensitivity C-reactive protein (hsCRP) and apolipoprotein (A1, B, B/A1 ratio), we can provide a well-rounded profiling of your heart health status.

Call or WhatsApp 6962 1362. Speak to our friendly medical team and make an appointment.

Our medical assessment includes:

  • Pre-assessment and medical review consultations with doctor
  • Professional Nurse evaluation
  • Height and weight
  • Body Mass Index (BMI)
  • Blood pressure monitoring including postural blood pressure monitoring

Cardiac Executive is the first of our heart health screening combinations. This screening is selected for busy executives with a focus on your cardiac health. From extensive cardiac blood screening to a treadmill stress test, this allows us to investigate any cardiac ischemia and the overall conditioning of your body.

Cardiac Gold Health Screening

Cardiac Gold is a popular cardiac screening that uses non-invasive 2-Dimensional (2D) echocardiography to produce cross-section views and observe real-time movement of heart structures to ascertain the heart function, heart valves function and any structural heart abnormalities without the risk of radiation from X-Rays.

Cardiac First Health Screening

First Class showcases CT Coronary Angiogram that allows detection of heart blockages in our coronary arteries without the need for invasive procedures.
