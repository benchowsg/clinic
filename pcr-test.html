COVID-19 PCR Swab Test

As a MOH-approved provider and licensed clinic, we provide pre-departure test (PDT) and pre-event test (PET) at A Healing Heart Medical Clinic for individuals aged 13 and above.

Companies and individuals seeking to take COVID-19 polymerase chain reaction (PCR) swab test can do so without prior approval from MOH. This includes COVID-19 Serology Test.

Travellers, however, will need to produce departure flight details for verification. Travellers are also responsible to check the testing window required by the country of destination.

In general, travellers must take the test within 48 to 72 hours before flight departs.

More outbound pre-departure information can be found on ICA's SafeTravel website.

Steps to Book Appointment (For PCR Test)

Step 1: Before you book an appointment, call 6962 1362 and speak to our clinic staff on test availability. Please highlight if you require an additional travel memo.

Step 2: Once you have verbally booked your preferred date and time, please email a copy of these documents to contact@ahealingheartmedical.com:

  • Passport (for all travellers)
  • Identity card (additional item for Singapore citizens, permanent residents, and long-term pass holders or work permit holders)
  • Vaccination report (via HealthHub using SingPass)
  • Departure flight details (for all travellers)

Step 3: Our clinic staff will confirm your appointment via email.

Step 4: On the day of appointment, please bring the following for verification:

  • Identity card
  • Passport (for all travellers)
  • Vaccination report
  • Departure flight details (for all travellers)

You will need to complete a pre-visit declaration form when you are at the clinic.

Please see a SASH/PHPC clinic doctor if you have acute respiratory symptoms (e.g. fever, cough, cold) on the day of appointment.

Approximate Duration

Approximately 15 to 20 minutes

When Result is Negative

We will send the results to your verified email address. If you have not received the email, it could be in your spam folder. Please print and bring the memo/test results when you travel.

For a normal PCR test, you will receive your results within 24 hours.

For an express PCR test, you will receive your results within 6 hours.

When Result is Positive

We will not be able to issue a memo for fitness to travel if your Pre-Departure COVID-19 PCR Swab Test result is positive. You will be contacted and arrangements for an urgent referral to NCID for subsequent care and management will be made.

When Result is Inconclusive

We will contact you if your result is inconclusive. A repeat test may be conducted, and it is chargeable. We shall not be held responsible or liable for any claims, damages, losses, expenses, costs, or liabilities whatsoever arising from this result should it affect your departure.
