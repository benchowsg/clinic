Metabolic Health

Weight Management Through Biological Optimization

Weight management is not a battle of willpower; it is a function of biology. At A Healing Heart, we focus on the metabolic markers—insulin, glucose, and lipids—to help you achieve a sustainable, healthy weight.

The Insulin-Weight Connection

When your metabolism is out of sync, your body enters a "Storage-Only" mode. We identify and treat the underlying hormonal triggers that cause weight plateaus.

Insulin Sensitivity Review

We measure fasting insulin and glucose to determine if your body is efficiently using fuel or storing it as visceral fat.

Fatty Liver Prevention

Metabolic health is closely tied to liver function. We screen for early signs of NAFLD (Non-alcoholic Fatty Liver Disease) as part of our weight management protocol.

Signs of Metabolic Dysfunction

  • Persistent Midsection Fat: Often an indicator of high cortisol or insulin resistance.
  • Energy Crashes: Difficulty maintaining energy levels after meals (Post-prandial fatigue).
  • Inability to Lose Weight: Despite strict calorie counting and exercise.

Metabolic Health: Clinical FAQ

Is weight management only about calories?

While calories matter, hormonal signaling (like insulin) dictates whether those calories are burned or stored. We focus on fixing the signaling first.

What is Metabolic Syndrome?

It is a cluster of conditions—increased blood pressure, high blood sugar, excess body fat around the waist, and abnormal cholesterol levels—that occur together, increasing your risk of heart disease.
