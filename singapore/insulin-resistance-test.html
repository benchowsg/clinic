Fasting Insulin & HOMA-IR Testing Singapore

Fasting insulin testing evaluates the exact volume of insulin your pancreas secretes to maintain stable blood sugar levels. At A Healing Heart Medical Clinic, we utilize the HOMA-IR (Homeostatic Model Assessment for Insulin Resistance) protocol to cross-reference fasting serum insulin with fasting plasma glucose. This calculation uncovers cellular insulin resistance years before traditional HbA1c or standard glucose checks register a diabetic trace.

Testing Protocol & What to Expect

Your testing window is managed entirely within our on-site laboratory framework to preserve sample integrity:

  • Booking: No referral letter from a polyclinic or general practitioner is required. Patients can self-refer directly for metabolic diagnostic auditing.
  • In-Clinic Routine: Phlebotomy is performed immediately upon arrival. The clinical draw takes less than 5 minutes.
  • Result Delivery: Once compiled, your diagnostic baseline analysis is transmitted securely to your verified contact profile. Critical variations are flagged for immediate consultation with Dr. Gerald Thang.
