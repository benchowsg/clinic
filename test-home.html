We Are

Advanced Physicians

Welcome to A Healing Heart Medical Clinic, where your healing journey begins.

We support patients with evolving, unclear, or complex symptoms that may not always be resolved in a standard consult.

Our team bridges the gap between general practice and specialist care, providing personalised assessments, ongoing guidance, and practical solutions tailored to your health needs.

We offer general care, preventive medicine, and intermediate care for people at different stages of health, taking the time to understand the full picture.

For urgent clarity, we provide rapid diagnostic tests for chest pain, suspected heart failure, haemoglobin, kidney health, and more, so you get answers without unnecessary delays.

Our role is to walk alongside you: tracking recovery, explaining results, and guiding your next steps with confidence.
