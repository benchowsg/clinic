Bridging the Gap in Your
Healthcare Journey

At A Healing Heart Medical, we recognize that some health concerns require more than a standard consultation, yet don't necessarily belong in a high-pressure hospital environment.

Our Intermediate Care Services are designed for every patient who seeks deeper clinical investigation, faster answers, and a dedicated partner to navigate complex health challenges.

The A Healing Heart Standard:
  • Extended clinical time for deep-dive history
  • On-site rapid diagnostic investigations
  • Integrated chronic disease navigation

When to Seek Intermediate Care

Persistent Symptoms

You have recurring discomfort, palpitations, or fatigue that "standard" checkups haven't fully resolved. We investigate the root cause, not just the symptoms.

Post-Hospital Support

Transitioning home after a hospital stay can be overwhelming. We manage your recovery, wound care, and medication reconciliation in a familiar clinic setting.

Care Coordination

Managing multiple specialists and prescriptions? We act as your central clinical coordinator to ensure your health plan is unified and safe.

Why Patients Choose Us

We provide specialist-level clinical depth with the accessibility and long-term relationship of a Primary Physician.

Rapid Diagnostic Clarity

Our clinic is equipped for rapid testing, providing critical blood markers and results in minutes or within the day.

Extended Consultations

Intermediate care requires time. We dedicate 20-40 minutes to understand your full medical narrative.

Maximizing Your Benefits

As a fully accredited clinic, we ensure your intermediate care remains accessible through appropriate subsidy schemes.

  • CHAS & Generation Card Subsidies
  • MediSave for Chronic Conditions
  • Care Coordination with Allied Health and Specialists
