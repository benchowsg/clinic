The Proactive

"I have a family history, or I haven't checked in a long time."

Our Focus: Early detection through high-sensitivity screening and cardiovascular risk stratification.

Newly Diagnosed

"I just found out my levels are high. I want to know if I need medication forever."

Our Focus: "Trial of Lifestyle" programs—optimizing diet and habits before committing to chronic medication.

The Plateau

"I've changed my diet and I exercise, but my numbers won't budge."

Our Focus: Investigating metabolic resistance and refining clinical strategies to break through the plateau.

The Managed

"I'm on medication, but I'm worried about side effects or my future heart health."

Our Focus: Medication optimization—ensuring the lowest effective dose with the highest heart protection.

Moving Beyond "Standard" Care

A blood pressure reading of 140/90 or a high LDL count is just a starting point. We look at the context of your life to create a sustainable plan.

Arterial Health Assessment

We don't just look at what's in your blood; we assess if it's actually causing plaque buildup in your arteries.

Rapid Metabolic Diagnostics

Our in-house blood tests allow us to monitor your progress and pivot your treatment plan in real-time.

Lifestyle Synergy

Personalized salt-reduction strategies, sleep hygiene, and stress management integrated into clinical care.
