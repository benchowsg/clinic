1. The Problem: The "TOFI" Trap

In Singapore, many of us are **TOFI**—Thin on the Outside, Fat on the Inside. You might look fit at the office, but your internal biology is struggling. This hidden visceral fat drives a full-body metabolic crisis that inflames your blood vessels, often years before a standard blood test shows an alarming number.

2. The Why: Insulin Resistance & Heart Friction

Think of insulin as a key that unlocks your cells so energy (glucose) can enter. When you become insulin resistant, those locks stop working. Your pancreas compensates by pumping out even more insulin to force the doors open.

While your blood sugar might stay "normal" for years, these high insulin levels trigger chronic inflammation in your artery walls. This accelerates plaque build-up—the very plaques that rupture and cause heart attacks.
