Before Starting a Statin

Use this 3-step clinical audit to prepare for your cholesterol consultation.

1. Do you have unexplained muscle aches or frequent gym-related soreness?

2. Have you ever been told your blood sugar is "borderline" or pre-diabetic?

3. Are you currently taking Red Yeast Rice or CoQ10 supplements?

Audit Complete: Your Readiness Profile

You have identified key baseline markers. Below is the clinical logic we will use during your consultation to tailor your vascular protection plan.

The Clinical "Why": Plaque vs. Pressure

If you are in the "Red Zone" (Ages 40–59), a statin is rarely about just lowering a number. It is about Plaque Stabilization.

Figure: Statins act as "glue" to prevent unstable plaque from rupturing.

Statins reduce vascular inflammation and "freeze" plaque in place. This prevents the primary cause of sudden heart attacks: plaque rupture. While lifestyle is the foundation, statins provide the structural reinforcement during the high-stress years of professional life.

Potential Concern Clinical Reality The Doctor's Insight

Your 3-Point Pre-Consult Checklist

Share these 3 details with Dr. Thang during your visit to optimize your plan:

  • ✅ Muscle Baseline: Differentiate between gym soreness and new aches.
  • ✅ Metabolic History: Monitor blood sugar if you have pre-diabetic markers.
  • ✅ Supplement Stack: Bring your bottles, especially Red Yeast Rice or CoQ10.

Do I have to take it forever?

Think of a statin like a metabolic seatbelt. If you achieve a Metabolic Pivot—losing 10-15% of body weight and maintaining a strict heart-healthy diet—we can trial a "Statin Holiday" under clinical supervision to see if your body can maintain the baseline on its own.
