The "Why": Beyond the Number

We do not treat a number on a cuff. We treat the mechanical failure that leads to stroke. The Hypertension Audit inspects your infrastructure for three specific triggers:

  • Heart Wall Thickness (LVH): Identifying if your heart muscle has become dangerously thick and stiff.
  • Arterial Flexibility: Checking if your "pipes" are still elastic or have turned into rigid, brittle tubes.
  • Micro-Vascular Health: Inspecting the kidney and brain "filters" for early pressure-scarring.

Lifestyle Trial vs. Mandatory Medication

To overcome a future stroke, we must be clinical about when to use medication. It is not a failure—it is a tool.

Lifestyle Trial is Viable IF:

Readings are 130-139 / 80-89 AND the Structural Audit confirms zero heart remodeling or arterial stiffening.

Medication is Mandatory IF:

Consistently 140/90+ OR ultrasound shows Remodeling has begun. At this stage, lifestyle changes cannot "un-stiffen" the heart fast enough to bypass stroke risk.

Audit FAQ

"Is high blood pressure always permanent?"

Not necessarily. If your audit shows the damage is early and reversible, we use medication as 'temporary scaffolding.' Once your lifestyle stabilizes and the heart wall thins back down, we can often taper the dosage.

"I have no family history. Why do I have high BP?"

In Singapore, high-stress environments and high-sodium diets can trigger hypertension even in the 'genetically lucky.' The audit reveals how your specific body is coping with these modern stressors.
