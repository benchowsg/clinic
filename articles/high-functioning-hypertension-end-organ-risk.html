2. Do you experience "Success Tax" signs? (Headaches, Tinnitus, or Brain Fog)

Perspective: The Genetic Infrastructure Risk

Why Your DNA Overrides Your Diet

Your history suggests that your risk is internal. For many Singaporeans, the liver is genetically programmed to overproduce LDL (sticky cholesterol) regardless of weight or exercise. This causes "Silent Sandblasting" of your heart and kidneys.

Because you are high-functioning, your body is incredibly good at compensating for high pressure. However, this compensation leads to Left Ventricular Hypertrophy (LVH)—a thickening of the heart wall that increases the risk of sudden events.

Clinical FAQ: Understanding Your Risk

Can I have high blood pressure without any symptoms?

Yes. Hypertension is the 'Silent Killer.' 53% of Singaporeans with high blood pressure only discover it during a clinical exam, often after years of silent organ strain.

What is 'High-Functioning' Hypertension?

It describes individuals who remain highly productive and feel 'fine' despite having dangerously high blood pressure. Their bodies compensate, masking the erosion of the heart, brain, and kidneys.

Why is the 40-59 age group called the 'Red Zone'?

NPHS data shows a sharp spike in chronic conditions in this decade. It is the period where lifestyle choices and genetic predispositions typically manifest as clinical disease.
