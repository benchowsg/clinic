Patient Expectation Management

This guide preserves all clinical value while addressing compliance issues—focusing on patient education rather than promotion. We have removed drug-class euphemisms and specific clinical instructions to comply with MOH standards.

Prescription Weight Management Treatment

We have replaced legacy terms with "prescription weight management treatment" throughout. This reflects MOH's explicit flagging of indirect advertising and ensures we are not using euphemisms for prescription medicines.

The Clinical Substance

The core of the clinical journey remains focused on three primary pillars:

  • The 5% Benchmark: The standard clinical milestone for assessing treatment response.
  • Titration Timeline: Understanding the phased approach to reaching therapeutic levels.
  • Body Recomposition: Explaining the biological difference between weight loss and fat loss.

Compliance & Tone

Educational Reframing: We have shifted away from "social media gap" angles to focus strictly on patient expectation management. This minimizes the risk of being scrutinized for intent to capture specific search traffic for indirect promotion.

Clean Clinical Instructions: We have removed references to injection sites or product-specific instructions, as these have no place in a public-facing educational article.

Medical Disclaimer

This content is educational and does not promote any specific medication. This statement serves as a clear indicator that the information is intended for expectation management and does not constitute a promotion of prescription medicines.
