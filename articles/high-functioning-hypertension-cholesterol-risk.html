Clinical FAQ

Can I be fit but have high cholesterol?
Yes. Genetic high cholesterol (Familial Hypercholesterolemia) is common. Your liver may produce excess LDL despite a perfect diet.

Why is undiagnosed hypertension so common?
High-functioning individuals often ignore subtle signs like brain fog or mild headaches, attributing them to work stress while the heart is under severe strain.
