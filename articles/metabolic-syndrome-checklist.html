The Syndrome X Checklist

Metabolic Syndrome is often "silent." Use this interactive tool to see if you meet the clinical criteria. Tap the cards that match your latest health report.

Markers Identified: 0 / 5

Select your markers to calculate your risk profile.

1

Abdominal Obesity

Excess visceral fat is a primary driver of systemic inflammation.

Threshold (Asian):
Men: > 90cm | Women: > 80cm
2

Elevated Blood Pressure

Consistent pressure against arterial walls, even at borderline levels.

Threshold:
130/85 mmHg or higher
3

High Triglycerides

The level of circulating fats in your blood from sugar and carbs.

Threshold:
1.7 mmol/L or higher
4

Low HDL Cholesterol

HDL is the "clean-up" cholesterol. Having too little increases risk.

Threshold:
Men: < 1.03 | Women: < 1.29 mmol/L
5

High Fasting Glucose

An early indicator of insulin resistance and pre-diabetes.

Threshold:
5.6 mmol/L or higher
