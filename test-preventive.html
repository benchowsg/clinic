Preventive Medicine:
Our Methodology

Proactive health is not a one-time event; it is a clinical strategy. At A Healing Heart Medical, we move beyond basic check-ups using a systematic "Data-First" model.

01

The Biomarker Baseline

The foundation of prevention lies in your Biomarkers. These are measurable internal signals—such as your cholesterol ratios, blood glucose (HbA1c), and inflammation markers (HSCRP)—that tell us how your body is actually performing.

Why we do this: Biomarkers reveal "silent" risks. You can feel perfectly fine while your arteries are under stress. By capturing these data points, we can intervene while a condition is still reversible.
02

Clinical Frequency

Frequency depends on your risk profile. We don't believe in "one size fits all" scheduling.

  • Annual Review: For healthy individuals to maintain their foundational baseline.
  • 3 to 6-Month Monitoring: For those with identified risks (like pre-diabetes or borderline hypertension) to ensure our interventions are working.
03

From Data to Prevention

We ensure care is truly preventive through Risk Stratification. We calculate your 10-year cardiovascular risk and metabolic trajectory, acting immediately with lifestyle medicine or targeted therapy long before it becomes a chronic disease.

Understanding Your 10-Year Risk

We don't just look at your results in isolation. We use your biomarkers to calculate your metabolic trajectory. By identifying risks while they are still 'silent,' we can implement lifestyle medicine that prevents chronic disease from ever developing.

Our Risk Triage Framework

How we use your data to build your personalized 10-year health roadmap.

Risk Category 10-Year Risk Score Primary Focus Intervention Strategy
Low Risk Below 5% Baseline Maintenance Annual biomarker tracking and longevity optimization.
Borderline 5.0% - 7.4% Early Trajectory Shift Lifestyle & nutritional therapy to "bend the curve" back to low risk.
Intermediate 7.5% - 19.9% Targeted Prevention Aggressive lifestyle medicine plus targeted pharmacological support.
High Risk 20% and above Crisis Prevention Intensive clinical management to stabilize biomarkers and prevent cardiac events.

*Risk scores are calculated using the ASCVD (Atherosclerotic Cardiovascular Disease) Estimator and clinical biomarker data.
