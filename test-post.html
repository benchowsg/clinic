The Bridge to Recovery

Hospital discharge is not the end of treatment—it is the start of recovery. We provide the Intermediate Care necessary to bridge the gap between acute hospital wards and your return to daily life.

Book a Recovery Assessment

Integrated Recovery Methodology

Multi-disciplinary care focused on functional restoration and clinical safety.

Advanced Wound Care

Managed by senior surgical nurses. We handle complex dressings, suture removal, and aggressive infection monitoring to ensure tissue heals without complication.

Cardiac Reconditioning

Post-surgical stamina must be rebuilt safely. We monitor hemodynamic response to ensure your heart adapts to activity without undue stress.

Functional Physiotherapy

Moving is medicine. Our focus is on functional strength—climbing stairs, standing balance, and building the stamina needed for independence at home.

Metabolic Calibration

We manage post-discharge electrolyte imbalances and adjust medications through rapid diagnostic blood tests, preventing avoidable re-hospitalization.

Psychological Resilience

Acknowledge and treat the "Post-Hospital Blues." We provide psychological support for both patients and caregivers to manage the mental toll of chronic recovery.

The Speed Factor

Restructured hospital appointments can be weeks away. We aim to see discharge patients within 48-72 hours to maintain recovery momentum.

The Critical First 30 Days

Most recovery complications occur shortly after discharge. Our roadmap focuses on the four areas that matter most to patients and their families.

"Is This Normal?" Monitoring

Early detection of fluid retention, wound discharge, or unusual fatigue. We provide a professional second opinion when you are unsure of a symptom.

Medication Reconciliation

Reviewing new hospital prescriptions alongside existing medications to prevent polypharmacy risks and adverse drug reactions.

Caregiver Empowerment

We support the family. Our team provides guidance on home care, mobility assistance, and clinical warning signs for the home environment.

The Recovery Quarterback

In the acute phase, specialists focus on the procedure. In the recovery phase, you need someone to focus on You.

A Healing Heart Medical coordinates between your hospital specialists, surgical nurses, and allied health team. We ensure no part of your health "falls through the cracks" during the transition home.
