Preventive health focuses on staying well, not just treating illness.

At A Healing Heart Medical, we support individuals through early detection, screening, and vaccination to reduce long-term health risks.

From lifestyle risk reviews to age-appropriate cancer screening and immunisations, we offer stepwise evaluations that guide your next steps clearly.

Preventive care also includes everyday habits like staying active, eating well, and attending regular check-ups. These small, consistent choices can make a big difference in your long-term wellbeing.

Osteopenia and Osteoporosis

Osteopenia is a condition with low bone density that increases the risk of fractures and osteoporosis. Though it may not have symptoms, aging, genetics, and lifestyle choices can lead to osteopenia. It can be diagnosed and treated with lifestyle changes, medications, and supplements. Prevention and management are important to promote bone health and prevent fractures.

Hypertension

Hypertension, or high blood pressure, can lead to serious health problems such as heart disease and stroke. It can often be prevented or managed through lifestyle changes such as maintaining a healthy weight, reducing salt intake, and getting regular exercise. Talk to us about steps you can take to manage this condition and protect your long-term health.

Hyperlipidemia

Hyperlipidemia, or high cholesterol, is a common condition that can increase the risk of heart disease and stroke. Diet and exercise play an important role in managing cholesterol levels, but medication may also be necessary. Let's take progressive steps to manage hyperlipidemia and set long-term goals.

Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic disease that affects how your body uses blood sugar. Without proper management, it can lead to serious complications such as heart disease, nerve damage, and kidney failure. Don't hesitate to contact us to learn more about how we can help you manage your diabetes and reduce the risk of complications.

Vaccinations

Vaccinations are a safe and effective way to protect against infectious diseases such as the influenza, pneumococcal, and hepatitis. By getting vaccinated, you not only protect yourself but also help prevent the spread of disease to others. Speak to us about vaccines that are recommended for you.
