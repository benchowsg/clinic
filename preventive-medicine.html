Preventive Medicine

We believe in guiding our patients in Preventive Medicine that goes beyond the usual annual health screening.

Individualised Screening

The keystone of Preventive Medicine at A Healing Heart lies in our strength in guiding and customising individualised screening for our patients. You can be assured of appropriate and targeted screening that goes beyond the usual annual health screening. This means you avoid paying for unnecessary tests.


Preventive Cardiology

We also offer preventive cardiology for different cardiovascular risk groups and seek to set our patients on a path to minimise their cardiovascular risk.


Treatment of Osteoporosis and Prevention of Osteoporosis/Osteopaenia

With our aging population, prevention of osteoporosis/osteopaenia is of utmost importance in reducing risk of osteoporosis related fractures and resulting disability. At A Healing Heart, our advanced physicians are equipped with the knowledge of using different treatment modalities including intravenous bisphosphonates.

We will be here to monitor your progress and also assist you with formulating an appropriate exercise program to maximise your potential bone mass recovery.


Vaccinations

Prevention is better than cure, and this will always be true. We provide comprehensive vaccinations for adults as well. Check with us for available vaccinations.
