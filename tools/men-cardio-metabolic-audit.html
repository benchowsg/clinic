Zone 1: The Slow Burn

Zone 2: The Inflammation Crossover

CRITICAL WINDOW (Ages 45–58)

The "Middle-Age Spread." Visceral fat begins to drive Silent Arterial Inflammation. This is the decade where cardiovascular risk officially crosses the threshold of hormonal protection.

Zone 3: The Vulnerability Gap

VASCULAR AGING (Ages 60+)

Melatonin and DHEA-S hit their "floor." Without these regenerative markers, the heart cannot fully recover from daily stress, leaving the arteries vulnerable to plaque progression.
