The Singapore BMR & TDEE Calculator

Weight management isn't a one-size-fits-all equation. Your **Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)** represents the absolute minimum energy your body burns at rest just to keep you alive and your organs functioning.

In our Asian context, factors like body composition, ambient humidity, and local activity patterns (like walking to MRT stations vs. desk-bound lifestyles) heavily impact our real daily caloric needs. Estimate your baseline and Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) below.
