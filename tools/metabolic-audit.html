Why the 0.5 Ratio?

Weight alone is a poor predictor of heart health. Visceral fat—the toxic fat wrapped around your organs—is the primary driver of arterial inflammation. The Waist-to-Height Ratio is a clinical gold standard for identifying "Skinny-Fat" (TOFI) risks that standard BMI misses.

The ApoB Connection

If your audit shows a high ratio, it often correlates with a high ApoB particle count. These are the lethal particles that burrow into artery walls. Identifying this early allows for Vascular Decompression—protecting your heart before damage occurs.
