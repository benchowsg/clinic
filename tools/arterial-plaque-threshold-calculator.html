Vascular Science
The Danger of Non-Calcified Soft Plaque

Standard imaging tracks calcified (hardened) plaque, which represents old, stabilized vascular scars. However, sudden ischemic cardiac events are overwhelmingly driven by the rupture of unstable, lipid-rich soft plaque. This calculator tracks the velocity at which these silent deposits accumulate under high metabolic pressure.
