What is "Soft Plaque"?

Think of plaque like wax building up inside a pipe. "Hard" plaque is old and scarred over, but "soft" plaque is greasy, unstable, and hidden inside the lining of your arteries. Because it is soft, standard health checks like basic treadmills often miss it entirely until it causes issues.

Why Belly Weight Matters to Your Heart

Genetics sets the baseline, but your metabolism acts as the driver. Carrying extra weight around your midsection, or having unbalanced blood sugar, irritates your blood vessels. This makes it much easier for regular cholesterol to get trapped in your arterial walls and turn into plaque.
