Understanding the 3 Metabolic Crossover Points

In men's health, the risk of cardiovascular events isn't a linear progression—it is often triggered by specific hormonal "handoffs."

Age 48

The DHEA-S Drop: Insulin resistance begins to override DHEA-S, signaling a decline in cellular repair capacity.

Age 52

The Estrogen Shift: Visceral estrogen increases, often accelerating abdominal fat gain and arterial inflammation.

Age 60

The Recovery Floor: Melatonin reaches its nadir, leading to poor sleep quality and higher night-time cardiovascular stress.

Hormone & Metabolic Audit FAQ

What is the connection between Insulin and Heart Health?
High insulin levels (Insulin Resistance) lead to arterial stiffness and inflammation, which are primary precursors to cardiovascular disease in men.

Can hormonal decline be reversed?
While aging is natural, the "rate" of decline can be optimized through targeted clinical audits, lifestyle shifts, and medical intervention to protect the heart.

Is this audit covered by MediSave?
Currently, hormone audits are not claimable under CDMP. However, thyroid markers will be included in the national framework starting in 2027.
