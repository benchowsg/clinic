Calcium (CAC) Scoring

A 10-minute CT scan that identifies calcified plaque. Especially vital for those over 79 or those with "Borderline" results, as a Score of 0 significantly lowers the need for aggressive medication.

Lp(a) Genetic Screening

A "sticky" genetic protein not found in standard cholesterol panels. 2026 guidelines recommend checking this **once in a lifetime** to identify inherited risk that lifestyle alone cannot solve.

The "Asian Phenotype" & Metabolic Risk

In Singapore, a "normal" appearance can be deceiving. Research confirms that Asian populations face significantly higher risks at a lower BMI.
