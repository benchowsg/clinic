Moving Beyond Probability

For those over 80 or those with borderline results, 2026 guidelines recommend looking at subclinical anatomy.

Calcium (CAC) Scoring

A specialized CT scan that detects actual calcified plaque in your arteries. A Score of 0 provides the "Power of Zero," often allowing older patients to avoid unnecessary medication.

Lp(a) Genetic Testing

Lipoprotein(a) is a "sticky" genetic protein that standard cholesterol tests miss. 2026 guidelines suggest testing this **once in a lifetime** to uncover silent inherited risk, especially in South Asian populations.

The "Asian Phenotype" & Risk

In Singapore, a "normal" appearance can be deceiving. Research confirms Asians face higher risks at a lower BMI.
