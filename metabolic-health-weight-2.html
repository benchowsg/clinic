Metabolic Health: Clinical FAQ

Is weight management only about calories?

While calories matter, hormonal signaling (like insulin) dictates whether those calories are burned or stored. We focus on fixing the signaling first.

What is Metabolic Syndrome?

It is a cluster of conditions (increased blood pressure, high blood sugar, excess body fat around the waist, and abnormal cholesterol levels) that occur together, increasing your risk of heart disease.

What is Metabolic Flexibility?

Metabolic flexibility is the body's ability to efficiently switch between burning carbohydrates and burning fat for fuel. Poor flexibility is often a root cause of weight plateaus.

How does insulin resistance prevent weight loss?

High insulin levels act as a "fat-storage" signal. When the body is insulin resistant, insulin stays high, effectively locking fat cells and making it difficult to lose weight despite calorie restriction.
