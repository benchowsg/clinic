Scientific Foundation

The A Healing Heart Health Estimator utilizes the ASCVD (Atherosclerotic Cardiovascular Disease) Risk Algorithm. This model was developed by the American College of Cardiology (ACC) and the American Heart Association (AHA) to identify individuals who may benefit from preventive therapies.

Clinical References
  • 1. Goff DC Jr, et al. 2013 ACC/AHA Guideline on the Assessment of Cardiovascular Risk. Circulation.
  • 2. Grundy SM, et al. 2018 AHA/ACC Guideline on the Management of Blood Cholesterol. Journal of the American College of Cardiology.
Regional Considerations

While highly validated for adults aged 20-79, this estimator is a screening tool. For the Singapore population, factors such as family history and specific ethnic risk enhancers are vital considerations that Dr. Gerald Thang integrates during clinical consultations.
