General Health Services

To better serve our community, we have General Health Services at affordable rates.

We believe in taking care of all aspects of our patientsâ€™ health besides Intermediate Care Services — Preventive Medicine, General Cardiology Care and Post-Hospitalisation Care.

If patients require more care and attention in their general health, we can intensify care and treatment at the clinic. We facilitate investigations with our specialist partners closely to ensure patients get the necessary treatments as soon as possible.

List of General Health Services includes but not limited to the following:

  1. Asthma
  2. Allergy
  3. Bronchitis
  4. Pneumonia
  5. Gastritis/gastric reflux symptoms
  6. Gastroenteritis
  7. Chronic constipation
  8. Thyroid disease
  9. Hypertension
  10. Hyperlipidaemia
  11. Diabetes
  12. Urinary tract infection
  13. Post-COVID vaccination antibodies level testing
  14. Vaccinations - Hepatitis B, Pneumovax 23, Influenza Fluarix Tetra, Prevenar 13, etc.
  15. PR application, employment, work permits
  16. Domestic worker health screening for renewal

We are CHAS accredited. Pioneer and Merdeka Generation, Blue/Orange/Green CHAS card holders can enquire about subsidised rates at the clinic. We have other General Health Services available, please check with our friendly staff to find out more.


We are not a Swab and Send Home (SASH) clinic or Public Health Preparedness Clinic (PHPC), and will not be able to provide subsidised rates for patients coming to us for ARI (Acute Respiratory Infection). For details on PHPC, please visit Flu Go Where website.
