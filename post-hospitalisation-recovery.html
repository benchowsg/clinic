Restructured hospital appointments can be weeks away. We can bridge the gap after your discharge to maintain recovery momentum.

The Critical First 30 Days

Most recovery complications occur shortly after discharge. Our roadmap focuses on the four areas that matter most to patients and their families.

"Is This Normal?" Monitoring

Early detection of fluid retention, wound discharge, or unusual fatigue. We provide a professional second opinion when you are unsure of a symptom.

Medication Reconciliation

Reviewing new hospital prescriptions alongside existing medications to prevent polypharmacy risks and adverse drug reactions, especially in elderly patients.

Caregiver Empowerment

We support the family. Our team provides guidance and coordinate home physiotherapy, in-clinic rapid diagnostics, and visit to specialists.

The Recovery Quarterback

In the acute phase, specialists focus on the procedure. In the recovery phase after hospitalisation, you need an experienced Principal Physician to focus on You.

A Healing Heart Medical coordinates between your private specialists, physiotherapists, psychologist, and allied health team. We ensure no part of your health "falls through the cracks" during your recovery.
