Cardiology

We are able to handle General Cardiology cases and we work closely together with our specialist colleagues to provide cardiology investigations at certified centres.

Partnerships

Our advanced physicians handle General Cardiology cases and assure quality intermediate level cardiology care.

We also work closely with our specialist colleagues to provide cardiology investigations at certified centres. Our close working relationships with tertiary care facilities will ensure that you will get rapid intervention if you should require it.


Types of Intermediate Level Cardiology Care

Quality intermediate level cardiology care includes management of:

  1. Ischaemic heart disease and coronary artery disease
  2. Atrial fibrillation including anticoagulation (see below)
  3. Chronic valvular heart disease monitoring
  4. Chronic heart failure
  5. Preventive cardiology
  6. Post hospitalisation cardiac reconditioning
  7. HIV Cardiomyopathy
  8. Exercise related hypertrophic cardiomyopathy
  9. Essential hypertension, secondary hypertension and resistant hypertension
  10. Familial hyperlipidaemia and familial hypertriglyceridemia
  11. Diabetes Mellitus Type 1 and 2 and adult onset diabetes type 1


Anticoagulation Service

we provide rapid anti coagulation testing services for monitoring of INR for warfarin and also our team will provide care for patients on Direct Oral AntiCoagulation or DOAC drugs like Apixaban, Dabigatran and Rivaroxaban. Anticoagulation is an important aspect of preventive medicine to reduce chances of stroke and also post hospitalisation medicine to prevent secondary strokes. You will need an experienced team which you will find at A Healing Heart to assist you with anticoagulation.
