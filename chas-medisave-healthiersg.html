CHAS, MediSave & HealthierSG Guide

Making Intermediate Care Accessible.

A Healing Heart Medical is a MediSave and CHAS-accredited clinic. We help you navigate national subsidies and MediSave claims to ensure your heart health remains affordable.

View Subsidies

CHAS Subsidies

Subsidies for common illnesses and chronic conditions. Applicable for Singaporeans with Blue, Orange, Green, and Pioneer/Merdeka Generation cards.

  • Acute Conditions
  • Chronic Diseases (CDMP)
  • Screen For Life

MediSave500/700

Use your MediSave to offset outpatient costs for chronic disease management (CDMP). Includes tests and medications.

  • Up to $500 or $700 yearly
  • 15% Co-payment applies
  • Can be used for dependents (See FAQ for more information)

Healthier SG

Enroll with us to unlock enhanced subsidies for chronic medications and fully-subsidised screenings. Government subsidies are for Singaporeans, and the amount depend on CHAS card colour.

  • Selected subsidised chronic medications
  • Fully-subsidised Vaccinations
  • No Cash Co-payment (Up to the annual limit)

Frequently Asked Questions

Which chronic conditions are covered under CDMP?

Chronic conditions are managed at our clinic include Hypertension (High Blood Pressure), Diabetes, Hyperlipidemia (High Cholesterol), Asthma, COPD, and Osteoporosis. The full list can be found here. Please note that our clinic cannot claim for Schizophrenia, Major Depression, Bipolar Disorder, and Anxiety.

Is there a limit to how much I can claim?

CHAS subsidies have annual claim limits based on the card type and condition complexity. MediSave (CDMP) has an annual limit of $500 (non-complex) or $700 (complex). You can track these balances in real-time on your HealthHub App.

Can I use my MediSave for my parents or children?

Yes. MediSave can be used for your immediate family members (spouse, children, parents, and grandparents). They must be Singapore Citizens or Permanent Residents.

  • Billed at Private rates.
  • Not eligible for Healthier SG subsidised drug tiers.

What do I need to bring for my first visit?

Please bring your NRIC and your physical or digital CHAS/Pioneer/Merdeka Generation card. Our staff will verify your subsidy eligibility via the national system instantly.

What happens if I hit my $500/$700 MediSave limit for the year?

The system will notify us if your limit has been reached when a claim is submitted. Any remaining balance for that visit must be settled via Cash, NETS, or Credit Card. You can check your remaining balance anytime on the CPF website or HealthHub app.

Can I use MediSave to pay for a Medical Report or Insurance Form?

No. Per MOH guidelines, MediSave and CHAS subsidies are for clinical treatment and diagnostics only. Administrative requests, such as formal medical reports for insurance or legal purposes, must be paid in cash/card at the clinic.

When will I see the 'Subsidised' price on my bill?

We practice Transparent Billing. Your itemised receipt will show the full private rate, followed by a deduction line showing the exact CHAS/MediSave subsidy applied, leaving you with only the final "Out-of-Pocket" amount to pay.
