Job Opening | Enrolled or Registered Nurse

We seek committed individuals with clinic experience of at least two years. Submit your application below if you fit the bill. Good luck!


Apply Now

Job Highlights

  • Compassionate and friendly working environment
  • Competitive salary and medical benefits
  • Easy commute and near Aljunied MRT station
  • 5.5-day work week
  • Not working on Sundays and public holidays
  • No night shifts
  • Dedicated lunch hour on weekdays

Job Description and Responsibilities

  • Performs Blood Glucose Monitoring, ECG Monitoring, Venepuncture, NGT Insertion, IV Fluid Therapy, IDC Insertion (Female), In-Out Catheter Insertion (Female) and Wound Dressing; IV Cannulation, IM Injection, Subcutaneous Injection, etc.
  • Maintain a clean and safe working environment
  • Controls crowd at the lobby and ensures safe management measures, including submitting an online COVID-19 declaration form
  • Assists doctor and be a chaperone when required
  • Supervises clinic assistant when dispensing medications

Requirements for Enrolled and Registered Nurse

  • Singaporean
  • Good communication skills in English and a second language (Chinese, Malay or Tamil); ability to communicate in dialects is advantageous
  • Good attitude and customer service skills
  • Comfortable in using computer applications
  • Willing to learn and work well in a team

Specific Requirements for Enrolled Nurse

  • At least 4 years of clinical experience as an Enrolled Nurse
  • Registered Practising Certificate (PC) issued by the Singapore Nursing Board
  • Valid Basic Cardiac Life Support (BCLS) and Automated External Defibrillator (AED) certification
  • NiTEC in Nursing certificate

Specific Requirements for Registered Nurse

  • At least 2 years of clinical experience as a Staff Nurse
  • Registered Practising Certificate (PC) issued by the Singapore Nursing Board
  • Valid Basic Cardiac Life Support (BCLS) and Automated External Defibrillator (AED) certification
  • Diploma, Post Graduate Diploma or Degree in Nursing
