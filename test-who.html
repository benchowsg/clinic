Who We Are

Bridging the gap in Singapore's healthcare through the Intermediate Care Model.

Our Mission

At A Healing Heart Medical Clinic, we have a dedicated team of medical professionals led by Dr. Gerald Thang, supported by highly trained nurses, psychologists, physiotherapists, and allied health specialists.

Together, we strive to provide exceptional care for our patients, utilizing the latest medical technologies and evidence-based practices. We are committed to improving the health and wellbeing of our patients through our compassionate and patient-centered approach to healthcare.

Our Dedicated Team

Meet the experts behind our Intermediate Care Model.

Dr Gerald Thang

Founder and Medical Director

Gerald founded A Healing Heart Medical with the vision of providing an ideal healing place for all our patients and caring not just our physical heart but also our emotional heart. Having worked in SGH, TTSH, KTPH, and Polyclinics, he recognized the need for an Intermediate Care Model in Singapore.

MBBS, NUS School of Medicine (2006)
• Diploma of Cardiology (Academy of Medicine, Singapore, and the Chapter of Cardiologists, 2016)
• MRCP and MRCPS (UK, 2019) • Member of the University of Glasgow (UK).

Ng Yan Ying

Principal Physiotherapist

Over 11 years of experience in both cardiorespiratory and musculoskeletal rehabilitation. She treats a wide range of conditions—from post-operative orthopedic care to chronic disease management.

Yan Ying specializes in dry needling, cancer rehabilitation, and personalized weight management programs aimed at long-term health and prevention.
Jaclyn Chow

Senior Physiotherapist

Over 15 years of experience in cardiorespiratory care. She specializes in cardiac and pulmonary rehabilitation, chest physiotherapy, and post-COVID rehab.

Jaclyn also supports patients with POTS and weight management through individualized programs focused on improving breathing, endurance, and overall well-being.
Amber Lim

Clinical Psychologist

Takes a warm, collaborative approach to helping individuals untangle stuck emotional patterns. Specialist in anxiety, depression, trauma, and chronic illness.

Amber works with adolescents, adults, parents, and caregivers, specialising in supporting clients through relationship difficulties and the psychological impact of chronic pain.
