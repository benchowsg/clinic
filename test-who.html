The Intermediate Care Model

Founded by Dr. Gerald Thang, A Healing Heart Medical addresses a critical need in Singapore’s healthcare landscape. We serve as the middle tier: providing more intensive resources than a standard GP, without the complexity and cost of a large hospital.

Our approach integrates advanced medical diagnostics with the "Irreplaceable Human Touch," guiding patients on an optimal healing path for both the physical and emotional heart.

Dr. Gerald Thang

Founder & Medical Director

A Vision for Holistic Healing

Dr. Gerald Thang graduated from the National University of Singapore in 2006. With a deep passion for Cardiology, he achieved a Diploma in Cardiology (Singapore) and earned Membership of the Royal Colleges of Physicians (UK) with distinction in PACES.

Having served in restructured hospitals (SGH, TTSH, KTPH) and community settings, Dr. Thang recognized that many patients fall into a "care gap"—they need more than primary care but don't require the acute intensity of a specialist clinic. This realization led to the birth of A Healing Heart Medical.
