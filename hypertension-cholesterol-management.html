Moving Beyond "Standard" Care

A blood pressure reading of 140/90 or a high LDL count is just a starting point. At A Healing Heart, we don't just "prescribe and dismiss." We look at the context of your life to create a sustainable plan.

  • Lifestyle Synergy: We integrate personalized salt-reduction and stress management strategies directly into your clinical care, aiming to reduce or minimise medication dependency where possible.
  • Medication Optimization: For those who tried lifestyle changes and still require treatment, we focus on finding the effective dose that provides the maximum cardiovascular protection with the fewest side effects.

Understanding Your Targets
