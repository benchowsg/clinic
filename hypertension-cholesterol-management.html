Intermediate Cardiovascular Diagnostics

Standard screenings often miss the "silent" drivers of heart disease. We offer advanced laboratory markers to provide a deeper understanding of your vascular health.

Advanced Lipid ProfilingUPON REQUEST

We measure ApoB (actual particle count) and Lp(a) (genetic risk) to identify heart-clogging risks that standard LDL tests cannot detect.

Vascular Inflammation

High-sensitivity C-Reactive Protein detects "silent" inflammation within the arterial walls, helping us predict and prevent future heart events.

Metabolic Resistance Screening

We assess Insulin Resistance to see if metabolic dysfunction is the hidden driver behind your stubborn high blood pressure.

Precision Medicine

By combining these intermediate markers, our physicians can move beyond "one-size-fits-all" care to create a cardiovascular plan tailored to your specific biology.
